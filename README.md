# General Counsel

**Location:** San Francisco (Onsite)  
**Type:** Full-time  
**Start Date:** ASAP

---

## What You'll Do

* Serve as the company's chief legal officer, advising on all legal matters
* Lead intellectual property strategy including patents, trade secrets, and licensing
* Structure and negotiate commercial agreements with enterprise and government customers
* Navigate regulatory and compliance requirements across aerospace, defense, and AI sectors
* Manage corporate governance, equity administration, and board matters
* Support fundraising activities including term sheet negotiation and due diligence
* Build and manage relationships with outside counsel across specialized areas
* Advise on employment law, immigration, and HR compliance matters

## What You Bring

**Strong experience with:**

* Technology transactions and IP licensing
* Patent strategy and prosecution oversight for deeptech/hardware companies
* Government contracts and defense procurement (FAR/DFARS)
* Enterprise SaaS and software licensing agreements
* Venture capital financings and corporate governance
* Export control regulations (ITAR, EAR)

**Familiarity with:**

* AI/ML regulatory landscape and emerging compliance requirements
* Open source licensing and compliance
* Data privacy regulations (GDPR, CCPA)
* Classified programs and security clearance requirements
* Hardware and embedded systems industry
* Startup equity structures and employee compensation

## What We're Looking For

We're looking for a general counsel who has built legal functions at deeptech companies — someone who understands the intersection of cutting-edge technology, intellectual property, and national security.

**You should have:**

* Track record advising hardware, aerospace, or AI companies
* Experience with government and defense sector customers
* Ability to move fast and provide practical, business-oriented legal guidance
* Comfort operating as a strategic advisor, not just a legal gatekeeper
* Excitement about building legal infrastructure from the ground up

**Requirements:**

* JD from a top law school
* Active bar membership in California (or ability to obtain)
* 8+ years of legal experience, with significant time at technology companies or advising tech clients
* Experience as in-house counsel at a venture-backed startup preferred
* Willingness to work startup hours, in-person at our San Francisco office
* Work authorization in the United States
